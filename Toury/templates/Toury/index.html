Location Aware Hands-free

As you walk around a specified location (a campus or an entire city), Glass will inform you of important information regarding the location you are currently standing in. As you look at the item of interest, Glass will show you more information as well as speak that information to you.

Google Glass App See for yourself.

Choose between a guided tour or a self guided tour. Search for destinations using only your voice. As you move your head around, markers will indicate the location of a point of interest. As you move closer to the destination, you will be told important information regarding that destination.

Create Tours Self-guided tours

Create a tour through this web application and allow users to explore your campus using the Google Glass app.

Choose points of interest around a campus or city. Just click on the map and enter the information that you want your user to see/hear as they walk in the location.
